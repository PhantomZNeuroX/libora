About

Reading is an essential skill that can have a profound impact on a child's development and academic success.It has numerous benefits, including improving communication skills, expanding vocabulary, and increasing knowledge and understanding of the world. Thus, it's important to introduce reading to children at a young age, but it's equally important to do so in a way that is engaging and effective. That's where Libora fits in!

Libora is a reading application that allows educators to create and share content with students, to help them start reading and develop strong reading skills. Libora’s advanced tracking system provides valuable insights for educators, helping them understand each child's reading habits and areas that need improvement.
